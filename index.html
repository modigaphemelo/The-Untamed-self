The Ghosts in the Room

This work does not happen in a vacuum. These thinkers haunt every page:

"We stand on shoulders that are still warm—the ghosts of those who refused to be tamed before us."

~ Field Note #31

Nietzsche

The hammer. He asks: "Is this making you stronger, or just more obedient?"

"One must still have chaos in oneself to be able to give birth to a dancing star."

Fanon

The wound. He reminds us: "The personal is political. Your pain has a history."

"Each generation must discover its mission, fulfill it or betray it."

Foucault

The jailer. He shows: "We build our own prisons, then call them freedom."

"People know what they do; frequently they know why they do what they do; but what they don't know is what what they do does."

They do not give answers. They give better questions.

"The untamed self is not born wild - it's what remains after everything domestic has been stripped away."

~ Field Note #58
